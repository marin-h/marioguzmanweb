Mario Guzmán — Experimental writer, media artist & creative robotics designer

Mario Guzmán is a Mexican writer and media artist with a focus on the links between culture, language, art, and education. Mario also explores the communication between human and non-human and the process of encoding perception and storytelling through technology. Outcomes include experimental cinema, non-linearity, mapping, generative literature, robotics and interactive media.

Expositions

X International Underground Cinema Festival / XIX Internacional Visual Arts Bienal, Bolivia / Digital Storytelling Anthology Itaú Award / Bienal de Arte Joven, Buenos Aires / Fulldome-UVM (Understanding Visual Music) / 105º Salón Nacional en Instalations and New M / Festival ZERO1, La Rochelle, France

Mario has a degree in Literature at the University of Buenos Aires and an MA in Technology and Aesthetics of Electronic Arts at UNTREF. He has also published articles in books like Visuality and device (s). Art and technique from a cultural perspective; And Movement Poetics. A perspective on Argentine experimental film and video.
