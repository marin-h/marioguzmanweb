Luke Fenech — Design & Direction
  
Luke Fenech is a London-based designer and art director with a focus on culture, luxury, fashion, interiors, retail and hospitality. Luke provides holistic design solutions for branding and identity with conceptual rigour and a refined aesthetic. Outcomes include identity, art direction, websites, packaging and editorial design.
