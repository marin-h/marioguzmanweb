Made In LA
Made in LA is a new Los Angeles based music group. Our mission for the Made in LA rebrand was very simple. We wanted to not only mark the beginning of a new artist but also to tell their story through a timeless icon. My goal was to create something which would represent their music, style and personality in a visually pleasing way. Made in LA is minimal, independant, defined and more importantly, new.
